Saithong Para Thai

About Us

Welcome to Saithong Para Thai, where the heart of Thailand meets the warmth of Virginia. “Saithong” means golden light — a symbol of joy and hospitality that inspires everything we do.

Located in Fredericksburg, VA, Saithong Para Thai is a beloved spot for authentic Thai cuisine. With a warm and inviting atmosphere, the restaurant offers flavorful dishes from traditional curries to stir-fries.

Famous Thai Dishes

Pad See Ew

A popular stir-fried noodle dish with wide rice noodles, soy sauce, Chinese broccoli, and egg. Slightly smoky and perfectly savory.

Yellow Curry

Rich and aromatic curry with potatoes and your choice of protein. Mild, creamy, and deeply flavorful.

Pad Thai

Stir-fried rice noodles with tamarind sauce, peanuts, egg, and bean sprouts. A sweet, tangy, and iconic Thai favorite.

Thai Tea / Cha Yen

Thai Tea

Rich Thai black tea with condensed milk served over ice.

Hot Thai Tea

A warm and aromatic version of the classic Thai tea.

Thai Ice Tea

Cool, sweet, creamy, and refreshing.
